# MediVerify User Manual

Welcome to MediVerify. This guide will help you navigate the system's core features.

## 🩺 For Doctors

### 1. Dashboard
- View a summary of your recent verification activities.
- Quick links to start a new upload.

### 2. Uploading Images
- Go to the **Upload** page.
- Drag and drop your medical image (JPEG/PNG, max 10MB).
- Click **Verify** to start the AI analysis.

### 3. Reviewing Results
- Once analysis is complete, you will see a verdict: **GENUINE** or **MANIPULATED**.
- **Confidence Score**: Indicates the AI's certainty.
- **Grad-CAM Heatmap**: Hover over or view the heatmap to see which regions the AI found suspicious.

### 4. History
- Access the **History** tab to view all your past verifications.
- Filter by date or search by filename.

---

## 🛡️ For Administrators

### 1. User Management
- View a list of all active Doctors in the system.
- Create new Doctor accounts.
- Enable or disable access for existing users.

### 2. System Analytics
- Monitor overall system accuracy.
- Track trends in detected manipulations.

### 3. Audit Logs
- View a permanent record of all actions taken in the system for security and compliance.

---

## ❓ Troubleshooting

- **Login Failed**: Ensure your credentials are correct and your account is active.
- **Upload Error**: Verify your file format is modern (JPEG/PNG) and under 10MB.
- **Prediction Slow**: Complex images may take up to 2 seconds to analyze.
